Corona Virus Preventation

Learn About It

Wear Your Mask

Wear your mask all the time while entering crowded place wherever you at outdoors and indoors place.

Wash Your Hand

If you have somehow touched body fluid such while coughing or sneezing, wash your hands immediately for 20 second with soap and water or clean with 60% alcohol.

Social Distancing

Keep the distance of at least 2 meters from other people and avoid the crowded area.
